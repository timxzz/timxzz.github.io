About Tim

Tim Xiao, also known as Zhenzhong Xiao, is an undergraduate computer science student at the University of Manchester. He is currently in industrial placement with Morgan Stanley, working with a global team to create tools for managing and monitoring the cloud platform that has a huge number of servers located around the world.

Just like lots of computer scientists, Tim has faith in technology, he believes technology won't solve all the problems for you, but will certainly take you closer to the answer.

At the moment, Tim is very interested in Machine Learning, a field that has amazing impacts on human's life.
